# TheRxOS Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to TheRxOS!

Thank you for choosing TheRxOS as your clinical opportunity management partner. This guide will walk you through the onboarding process to get your pharmacy up and running.

---

## What TheRxOS Does

TheRxOS scans your prescription claims data to identify:
- **Therapeutic Interchange Opportunities** - Switch to preferred formulary alternatives
- **Missing Therapy Opportunities** - Identify patients who may benefit from additional therapies
- **Optimization Opportunities** - Improve adherence and clinical outcomes

Our platform tracks each opportunity from identification through prescriber approval, helping you capture additional revenue while improving patient care.

## Onboarding Checklist

### Step 1: Sign Agreements
- [ ] Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
- [ ] Service Agreement

### Step 2: Provide Data Export
We need a prescription data export from your pharmacy management system containing:

| Required Field | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| Rx Number | Unique prescription identifier |
| Patient Full Name | Last, First format |
| Patient Date of Birth | MM/DD/YYYY |
| Date Written | Prescription date |
| DAW Code | Dispense As Written code |
| Dispensed Item Name | Medication name |
| Dispensed Item NDC | 11-digit NDC |
| Dispensed Quantity | Amount dispensed |
| Days Supply | Days the Rx will last |
| Dispensed AWP | Average Wholesale Price |
| Net Profit | Your profit per Rx |
| Patient Paid Amount | Copay/coinsurance |
| Primary Third Party BIN | Payer BIN |
| Primary Group Number | Insurance group |
| Primary Network Reimbursement | Payer reimbursement |
| Prescriber Full Name | Provider name |
| Prescriber Fax Number | For outreach |

**Export Instructions by PMS:**
- **PioneerRx**: Reports > Custom Reports > [TheRxOS Export Template]
- **Rx30**: Reports > Third Party > Claims Export
- **Liberty**: Analytics > Export > Claims Data
- **Other**: Contact us for specific instructions

### Step 3: Account Setup
Once we process your data, you'll receive:
- Login credentials for beta.therxos.com
- Initial opportunity report
- Training session scheduling link

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often should I send data exports?**
A: We recommend weekly exports for optimal opportunity identification. You can upload directly through the dashboard.

**Q: Is my patient data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and sign a BAA with every pharmacy.

**Q: How long until I see ROI?**
A: Most pharmacies see their first approved opportunities within the first week. Average ROI is 10-20x the monthly subscription.

**Q: Can I customize which opportunities I see?**
